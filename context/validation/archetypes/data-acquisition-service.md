## Data Acquisition Validation

Validate one fetch-to-persist path on deterministic inputs plus one replay or duplicate-suppression case. Source-specific quirks should be visible in the proof path.

Prefer recorded fixtures or deterministic sample responses when upstream systems are unstable.
